OUR STORY

Coach William Training was founded by a professional truck driver with decades of real-world commercial driving experience. His career in the trucking industry spans multiple roles — from hands-on mechanical work to life on the road and eventually teaching the next generation of drivers.

His experience includes:

Diesel Mechanic
Company Driver
Owner-Operator
Fleet Owner
OTR Driver Trainer
CDL School Instructor

He worked as a diesel mechanic before beginning his professional driving career, and over the years has developed practical teaching techniques that help students understand, remember, and confidently perform the CDL inspection.

OUR MISSION

The mission of Coach William Training is to help CDL students build confidence, reduce test anxiety, and pass their CDL exams using practical, organized instruction.

We understand the pressure that comes with the CDL Skills Test. Every professional driver started exactly where you are today — and we believe the right preparation can make all the difference between a nervous test day and a confident one.

"My goal isn't just to help you pass your CDL Skills Test. My goal is to help you become a safe, confident professional driver."

THE KIRKLAND RAPID RECALL METHOD™

The Kirkland Rapid Recall Method™ is one of the teaching techniques used within the app. It was developed from years of real-world CDL training and classroom instruction.

The method uses a multi-layered approach to build long-term memory and genuine understanding:

Repetition
Reverse Recall
Random Recall
Confidence Building

Rather than simply helping students memorize answers, The Kirkland Rapid Recall Method™ is designed to help students truly understand the inspection process. When you understand why each step matters, you don't just pass a test — you become a safer, more capable professional driver.

More Than Memorization

The goal is not simply to help students memorize answers, but to truly understand the inspection process and become safer professional drivers. Every lesson, quiz, recall mode, and coaching message is designed to build confidence through repetition, understanding, and encouragement.

Whether you're earning your first CDL or adding new endorsements, Coach William Training is here to help you succeed — one step at a time.
