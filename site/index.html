OUR MISSION

Coach William Training was founded by a professional truck driver with decades of real-world commercial driving experience — including years as an over-the-road CDL trainer and CDL school instructor.

Our mission is to help CDL students build confidence, reduce test anxiety, and pass their CDL exams using practical, organized instruction, step-by-step guidance, and proven memory techniques.

One of the teaching techniques used inside the app is The Kirkland Rapid Recall Method™, which strengthens memory through forward, backward, random, and pressure recall practice.

THE APP

Coach William CDL Coach is a mobile learning app designed to help CDL students prepare for the CDL Pre-Trip Inspection and Air Brake Test. It combines industry-standard inspection procedures with Coach William's unique teaching style, inspection flow, coaching methods, memory techniques, and confidence-building approach.

The Kirkland Rapid Recall Method™

One of the teaching techniques used within the app. It strengthens memory through repetition, reverse recall, random recall, and confidence-building techniques to improve long-term memory.

The goal is not simply to help students memorize answers, but to truly understand the inspection process and become safer professional drivers.
